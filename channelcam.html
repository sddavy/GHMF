Channel Cam

92.1 WGHN "The Channel" is a local radio station in Grand Haven, MI. They host live IP cameras in two locations in Grand Haven, one of which, the "Channel Cam", is located outside of the Lynne Sherwood waterfront stadium and can be panned either towards the mouth of the harbor or towards the Grand Haven Musical Fountain. When focused towards the Fountain, it can be used to view any one of the Fountains shows.

Although the camera can be viewed from the official WGHN website, the unwanted ads and unusual layout make viewing it difficult to enjoy viewing. To fix this problem, the live feed can be found below, and a simple version can be found at the button below.
